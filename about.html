About Us

Paul and Suzanne Bane - Fine Artists

Paul Bane

Fine Artist

Paul has been painting for as long as he can remember. His many trips to the seaside as a child have been an immense source of inspiration to him. His paintings portray the ever changing weather conditions. Huge dramatic skies, stormy seas or peaceful horizons, his paintings depict his love of light, colour and atmosphere.

Paulâ€™s Paintings are widely sought after and are held in many private collections. He has exhibited six times in the Laing Art Exhibition at Harrogate Gallery, Scarborough Gallery and the Mall Gallery London. He has also exhibited in Leeds Art Show. He was awarded winner of the best painting at the Guild of Visual Artists National Exhibition held in 1988.

Suzanne bane

Fine Artist

Suzanne spent her childhood drawing and painting. Following the completion of her Graphic Design and Advertising Degree, Suzanne worked in Design studios, Advertising Agencies, Greeting Card Companies, Illustration and print. In 2008 she decided to embark on a career as a full-time Artist.

About Us

Paul and Suzanne Bane are fine artists from Yorkshire. Paul stands on the York Shambles Market every Sunday where you can view and purchase our original paintings and signed limited prints. View our web gallery to gain a taste of our work and contact us via our contact page email if you have any enquires.
